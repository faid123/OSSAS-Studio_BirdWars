Game Name: Bird Wars - Last Flight
Genre: Action, Strategy

Description: Help General Cockadoodle to fight
the invading forces seeking to steal the eggs of innocent
bird kind everywhere. Bird Wars is an action strategy game
where players have to overwhelm the enemy by managing 
troops and resources to destroy the enemy tower in order to
achieve victory. It is a strategy game where you have to outsmart
the enemy's AI.
